Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the questions we're asked most often.

How old do I need to be to start driving lessons?

You can begin learning to drive once you have obtained the appropriate learner licence according to NSW road rules.

Do I need my own car?

No. We provide a fully insured, dual-control vehicle for all lessons.

How long is each driving lesson?

Most lessons are 60 minutes, although longer lessons can be arranged depending on your needs.

Can you help me prepare for my driving test?

Absolutely! Our instructors provide mock driving tests and teach the skills required to help you feel confident on test day.

What areas do you service?

We provide driving lessons throughout Ingleburn and surrounding Sydney suburbs.

How do I book a lesson?

You can fill out a form after selecting a package or you can contact us by phone, email or through the Contact Us page on our website.

What happens if I need to cancel?

Please let us know as early as possible. Our cancellation policy will be explained when you book your lesson via email.
